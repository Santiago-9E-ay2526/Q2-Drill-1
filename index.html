EMERGENCY GO BAG KIT

What to pack in your emergency kit

Three-day supply of non-perishable, calorie-dense foods and emergency water pouches. All items require no refrigeration, minimal preparation, and are packaged for long shelf life.

Comprehensive set of wound care materials—bandages, gauze pads, antiseptics, gloves, and essential tools—to handle common injuries until professional help is available.

Additional comfort and personal-use items—compact toys, quiet activities, stationery, and other small objects that provide distraction, familiarity, or emotional support.

Durable, easy-to-access items (flashlight, multitool, whistle, and backup batteries) to support safety, visibility, and basic problem-solving in crisis situations.
